Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026.

1. What we store on your device

Club Ten saves the following data in your browser's local storage (similar to cookies but never sent to any server):

  • Your chosen club
  • Your daily game results (which answers you found, lives remaining)
  • Your statistics: streak, games played, perfect games

This data stays entirely on your device. Clearing your browser's site data removes it permanently.

2. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how many people visit the site and which pages are most popular. Google Analytics collects anonymised information including your approximate location (country/city level), device type, browser, and pages visited. It does not collect your name or contact details.

See Google's Privacy Policy for full details.

3. Advertising

We display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies or similar technologies to show you ads based on your interests. You can review and adjust your ad settings at adssettings.google.com .

4. Cookies

Club Ten itself sets no first-party cookies. Google Analytics and Google AdSense may set third-party cookies on your device to function. A cookie consent banner will be shown before these services load, in compliance with UK GDPR and the ePrivacy Regulations.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties beyond the analytics and advertising providers described above.

6. Your rights (UK & EU residents)

Under UK GDPR and the EU GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or erasure of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (UK) or your national supervisory authority

Because all game data lives in your own browser, you can erase it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

7. Children

Club Ten is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Contact

Privacy queries: topclubten@gmail.com
