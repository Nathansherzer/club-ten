Objective

Each puzzle has ten hidden answers. Type a name and press Guess (or Enter) to submit it. Correct answers fill their slot with a green animation. Find all ten to win.

Lives

You have three lives (❤️❤️❤️). A wrong guess — one that isn't on the board — costs one life. Guessing something you've already found doesn't cost a life. Lose all three and the remaining answers are revealed.

Typing tips

  • A surname on its own is usually enough — Salah, Klopp, Wenger.
  • One-letter typos are forgiven — don't worry about perfect spelling.
  • Accented characters are handled — type Solskjaer or Solskjær, both work.
  • As you type, a dropdown of suggestions appears. Tap a name to submit it instantly. Picking a wrong suggestion still costs a life.

Streaks

Your streak increases by one each day you score 5 or more out of 10. Miss a day or score 4 or below and the streak resets to zero.

Sharing

After the game, tap Share result to copy a spoiler-free emoji grid (🟩 = found, 🟥 = missed) to your clipboard. On phones it opens the native share sheet.

Stats

All stats (streak, games played, perfect scores) are stored in your browser's local storage. They stay on the device you're playing on — no account, no cloud sync.

One puzzle per day

A new puzzle drops every day at midnight London time. Past puzzles are available to browse in the Archive, but answers are revealed — archive entries can't be played.
