What is Club Ten?

Club Ten is a free daily football trivia game. Each day you get a top-10 challenge about your chosen club — name the last ten managers, the all-time top scorers, the most-capped players, and more.

How it works

Pick your club once and the game remembers it. Every day at midnight London time a new puzzle drops. You have three lives — a wrong guess costs one. Your streak, games played, and perfect scores are all saved in your browser; no account needed.

The data

Every puzzle is researched and date-stamped. Answers include the season or date range through which they were verified, so you always know how current the data is. If you spot an error, please get in touch via the contact page.

A note on images

Club Ten is text-only — no club crests, kits, or player photos. This keeps the site free of football image licensing issues and loads fast on mobile.
